    FAQ

    PuckCity is a decentralized staking platform that allows users to stake tokens for their favorite NHL teams. Users can earn rewards in the form of PuckCity tokens for staking their team tokens.
    To stake your team tokens, select your desired team from the dropdown list on the "Team Tokens" section of the website. Once selected, enter the amount of tokens you wish to stake and click the "Stake" button.
    To claim your rewards, select your desired team from the dropdown list on the "Rewards" section of the website. Once selected, enter the amount of rewards you wish to claim and click the "Claim Rewards" button.

    Tutorial

    Learn how to use PuckCity with this step-by-step tutorial:

    1. Connect your wallet to the PuckCity website.
    2. Select the team you want to stake.
    3. Enter the amount of tokens you want to stake.
    4. Click the "Stake" button.
    5. Monitor your rewards on the "Rewards" section
    6. If you want to unstake your tokens, go to the "Staking" section and click the "Unstake" button. Your tokens will be returned to your wallet after a 24-hour cooldown period.
    7. You can claim your rewards anytime by clicking the "Claim Rewards" button in the "Rewards" section.
    8. If you have any questions or issues with the PuckCity platform, visit the "Contact" section to get in touch with our support team.
